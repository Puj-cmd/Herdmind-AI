HerdMind AI

About The App

HerdMind AI is an intelligent livestock management application developed to help farmers monitor animal health, reduce disease risks, and improve farm productivity. The application combines Artificial Intelligence, Weather Monitoring, Location Services, Animal Care Guidance, and Emergency Support into a single easy-to-use platform. It is designed especially for farmers who need quick and reliable information for maintaining healthy livestock.

Features

Disease Risk Prediction
Real-Time Weather Monitoring
Prediction History Tracking
Farmer Tips and Guidance
Animal Care Guide
Emergency Veterinary Contacts
Multi-Language Support
GPS Based Weather Information

Technology Used

  • Android Studio
  • Java
  • Firebase Firestore
  • OpenWeather API
  • Google Location Services
