Smart Livestock Monitoring and Disease Prediction System

About The App

HerdMind AI is an intelligent livestock management application developed to help farmers monitor animal health, reduce disease risks, and improve farm productivity. The application combines Artificial Intelligence, Real-Time Weather Monitoring, GPS Location Services, Animal Care Guidance, Emergency Support, and Prediction History into one easy-to-use platform. It is specially designed for farmers to make better decisions for livestock health and farm management.

Our Features

🐄 Disease Prediction

Predicts livestock disease risk using animal temperature, humidity, activity level and feeding information. The system classifies animals into Low Risk, Medium Risk and High Risk categories.

🌦 Real-Time Weather Monitoring

Uses GPS and OpenWeather API to provide live temperature, humidity and weather conditions for the farmer's current location.

📖 Animal Care Guide

Provides information about feeding, vaccination, hygiene practices, shelter management and livestock care.

🚨 Emergency Contacts

Provides quick access to veterinary hospitals, animal care helplines and emergency services.

💡 Farmer Tips

Offers useful tips on livestock health, nutrition, disease prevention and farm management.

📜 Prediction History

Stores previous predictions in Firebase Firestore so farmers can track animal health records over time.

🌐 Multi-Language Support

Supports multiple Indian languages to make the application easy to use for farmers from different regions.
